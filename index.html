Career Timeline

A chronological journey through the remarkable career of MS Dhoni.

ODI Debut

December 23, 2004

Made his One Day International debut for India against Bangladesh.

Maiden ODI Century

April 5, 2005

Scored a blistering 148 against Pakistan in Visakhapatnam, announcing his arrival on the world stage.

T20 World Cup Victory

September 24, 2007

Led a young Indian team to victory in the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in South Africa.

ICC ODI World Cup Glory

April 2, 2011

Hit the iconic six to win the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup for India after 28 years.

Champions Trophy Triumph

June 23, 2013

Became the only captain to win all three major ICC trophies: T20 World Cup, ODI World Cup, and Champions Trophy.

International Retirement

August 15, 2020

Announced his retirement from all forms of international cricket, ending a legendary career.
